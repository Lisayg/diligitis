ABOUT

Est. 2017

Diligitis is a consulting & design lab that deals with making a beautiful lifestyle. The idea was conceived at FB She Means Business and made a team at the Artience Creation Challenge. This platform focuses on global lifestyle and entertainment projects that foster cultural enrichment and international connection. The site hosts creative projects across various mediums, covering art, media, and events to engage and inspire a global audience.

LisayG, the founder and CEO, debuted in Japan and has been a design guru with 25 years of experience in the San Francisco Bay Area, Greater China, and Europe. Dr.MOK, a science advisor, is best known for winning the first in FameLab Korea and a Ph.D. in Developmental Genetics at KAIST. Chiaki N. is a communication specialist, she promotes global media relations.
